Welcome to my personal blog.

I use this space to write about software engineering, frontend development,
AI-assisted workflows, and the small decisions that shape real products. Some
posts are technical deep dives. Others are shorter notes, experiments, or
lessons learned while building.

This site is intentionally personal. I am less interested in writing generic
tutorials and more interested in documenting how I think, what I am working on,
what changed my mind, and what was worth keeping after the first version
shipped.

If you are interested in practical engineering, product-minded development, and
tools that improve how we work, you will likely find something useful here.

## What you will find here

- [Engineering](/engineering): architecture, tradeoffs, debugging notes, and
  lessons from shipping software.
- [Frontend](/frontend): React, Next.js, UI systems, performance, and developer
  experience on the web.
- [Mobile](/mobile): iOS, Android, React Native, cross-platform architecture,
  performance, and mobile product engineering.
- [AI Workflows](/ai-workflows): practical notes on using AI tools in real
  product and coding workflows.
- [Projects](/projects): build logs, release notes, retrospectives, and what I
  learned from making things.
- [Notes](/notes): shorter thoughts, small experiments, and ideas that are
  useful before they become full essays.
